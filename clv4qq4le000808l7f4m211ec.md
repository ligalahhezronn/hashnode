Videos can captivate and inform.

```xml
<video></video>
```

Just like images, videos are embedded (or linked) into a web page.

```xml
<video>
<source src="file.mp4"
type="video/mp4">
</video>
```

```xml
<video> 
    <source src="videofile.mp4" type="video/mp4"> 
    <source src="videofile.webm" type="video/webm"> 
    Video not supported     
</video>
```
